Michigan Dining App

During my Winter 2018 semester I was paired with a group of other students to analyze the usability of the Dining portion of the Michigan App. Throughout this process we conducted user tests and interviews, administered surveys, and created user personas to identify the key problems with the Dining App. In the end we focused on the lack of fluidity in the application and conducted A/B testing on the Location aspect using paper prototypes that tested the ability for users to decide if they wanted to leave the Michigan App to go to Google Maps or not. In line with our hypothesis, most users said that they preferred being notified that they were about to leave the application.
